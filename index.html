FlaminGokite

Kitesurf eco station at Aliki beach, Limnos island, Greece

FlaminGokite is the first kitesurf-only station on the island of Limnos. It's situated at Aliki beach, North from the Keros bay.

Our idea is to keep things simple and functional - certified experienced instructors, new Cabrinha equipment and be one with the nature. We love strapless and foil kitesurfing and we offer courses / rentals for both.

about our idea

We believe that good wind conditions, quality equipment, and responsible instructors with a personal attitude towards our guests are key elements for a successful kitesurfing station. 
Being one with our surroundings is important since Aliki beach is located in a highly protected area part of NATURA 2000, next to Aliki lake - home to the pink flamingos, and we try to be as eco-friendly as possible.
Our goal is to help you progress as a kitesurfer and relax with the nature while listening to good music, enjoying the sunsets, and living in the present moment, regardless of who you are and what you do in life.
